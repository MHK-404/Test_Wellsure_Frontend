Welcome to Wellsure

Wellsure is your personal guide to understanding and improving your health risks. By analyzing your physical health, mental well-being, daily habits, and environment, we generate a custom health risk score along with science-backed, practical recommendations to enhance your lifestyle. Plus, our healthcare cost calculator estimates your potential out-of-pocket expenses after insurance, based on your annual income and risk score.

Let's take the first step toward a healthier, happier you!
